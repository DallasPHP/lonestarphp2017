Venue

This year the Lone Star PHP Conference is being held at the Addison Conference Centre in Addison, TX from April 7th — 9th. Conveniently located near the Dallas North Tollway, the Centre includes the state of the art Addison Theatre Centre, the award-winning Addison Conference Centre and historic Stone Cottage.

For more information on the Addison Conference Centre and surrounding area, go to their site.

Addison Conference and Theatre Centre
15650 Addison Rd,
Addison, TX 75001

From DFW Airport

  1. Take 121 North out of the airport.
  2. Head east on 635 for about 14 miles.
  3. Take exit 22c onto Dallas North Tollway heading north.
  4. Exit Belt Line Rd/Arapaho Rd.
  5. Turn left on Arapaho. Then turn right on Quorum Dr.
  6. Follow the circle and go west on Addison Circle.

From Love Field Airport

  1. Turn left out of the airport, heading east on Mockingbird Ln.
  2. Merge onto North Dallas Tollway heading north for 10 miles.
  3. Exit Belt Line Rd/Arapaho Rd.
  4. Turn left on Arapaho. Then turn right on Quorum Dr.
  5. Follow the circle and go west on Addison Circle.
